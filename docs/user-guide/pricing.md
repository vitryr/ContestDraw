## How Credits Work

ContestDraw uses a **credit-based system** where you pay for what you use:

- ‚úÖ **One-time purchases**: Buy credits when you need them
- ‚úÖ **No expiration**: Credits never expire
- ‚úÖ **Transparent pricing**: Know exactly what each draw costs
- ‚úÖ **Pay per draw**: Only charged when executing draws, not creating them

### Credit Usage

**Credits are deducted when:**
- Executing a contest draw
- Re-executing a draw (if you need to pick new winners)
- Using advanced filters

**Credits are NOT used for:**
- Creating draft draws
- Syncing participants
- Viewing results
- Cancelling draws
- Connecting social accounts

## Subscription Management

### Upgrading

**From Free to Pro/Business:**
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Billing
2. Click "Upgrade"
3. Select your plan
4. Enter payment details
5. Subscription starts immediately

**Pro-rated billing:** If you upgrade mid-month, you'll only pay for the remaining days.

### Downgrading

**From Pro/Business to Free:**
1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Pro features remain active until end of billing period
5. Unused monthly credits are forfeited (one-time purchased credits remain)

### Cancelling

- No cancellation fees
- Cancel anytime before next billing cycle
- Unused monthly credits are forfeited
- Purchased credits remain available

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Do credits expire?**
A: No! Purchased credits never expire. Only monthly subscription credits are forfeited if unused.

**Q: Can I transfer credits to another account?**
A: No, credits are non-transferable between accounts.

**Q: What happens if I run out of credits mid-draw?**
A: You'll be prompted to purchase more credits before the draw executes. The draw won't start without sufficient credits.

**Q: Can I get a discount for non-profits?**
A: Yes! Non-profits and educational institutions receive a 50% discount. Contact us with proof of status.

### Billing

**Q: How are monthly credits calculated?**
A: Credits are added on your billing date (e.g., if you subscribe on the 15th, you get credits on the 15th of each month).

**Q: Do monthly credits roll over?**
A: Yes, for Pro and Business tiers. Unused monthly credits roll over for up to 12 months.

**Q: Can I change my payment method?**
A: Yes, go to Settings ‚Üí Billing ‚Üí Payment Methods.

**Q: What currency are prices in?**
A: All prices are in EUR (‚Ç¨). If you pay in another currency, your bank's exchange rate applies.

### Refunds

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the draw results?**
A: Unfortunately, we can't refund credits for successful draws. However, if there was a technical issue, contact support.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I accidentally clicked "Execute Draw"?**
A: No, there's a confirmation step to prevent accidents. Once executed, draws cannot be refunded.

**Q: What if my winner doesn't respond?**
A: You can run a "redraw" to select a new winner at 50% of the original cost.
