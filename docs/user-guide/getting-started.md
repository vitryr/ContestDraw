# Getting Started with Cleack

Welcome to Cleack! This guide will help you run your first social media contest in minutes.

## What is Cleack?

Cleack is a platform that helps you run fair, transparent, and automated contests on social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, and YouTube. Our advanced filtering system ensures only genuine, qualified participants are included in your draws.

## Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [https://cleack.io](https://cleack.io)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner
3. Fill in your details:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First and Last Name
   - Company (optional)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email and click the verification link

**üéÅ Bonus:** New accounts receive 10 free credits to get started!

### Step 2: Connect Your Social Media Account

Before running a draw, you need to connect the social media account where your contest is hosted.

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Social Connections**
2. Click **"Connect"** next to your platform (e.g., Instagram)
3. You'll be redirected to authorize Cleack
4. Grant the necessary permissions:
   - Read your posts
   - Read comments
   - Read follower information
5. You'll be redirected back to Cleack

**Why do we need these permissions?**
- To fetch comments from your contest post
- To verify if participants follow your account
- To ensure fair and accurate draws

### Step 3: Create Your First Cleack

1. Click **"New Draw"** from your dashboard
2. Fill in the draw details:

#### Basic Information
- **Title**: Give your contest a memorable name (e.g., "Win a MacBook Pro!")
- **Description**: Optional description for your records
- **Platform**: Select where your contest is hosted
- **Post URL**: Paste the link to your contest post

#### Draw Settings
- **Number of Winners**: How many winners to select (1-100)
- **Draw Date**: Schedule for later or draw immediately

#### Filters (Optional but Recommended)
Configure filters to ensure fair participation:

- ‚úÖ **Exclude Multiple Comments**: Prevent users from commenting multiple times
- üë• **Minimum Followers**: Set a minimum follower count (e.g., 100)
- üíô **Must Follow Account**: Require participants to follow you
- üè∑Ô∏è **Must Tag Friends**: Require tagging friends (specify how many)
- ‚ù§Ô∏è **Must Like Post**: Require participants to have liked the post
- üö´ **Exclude Business Accounts**: Only allow personal accounts
- üìÖ **Minimum Account Age**: Require accounts older than X days
- üîí **Exclude Keywords**: Block comments containing spam keywords

### Step 4: Review and Execute

1. Click **"Create Draw"**
2. Review the cost in credits (typically 5-20 credits depending on filters)
3. Click **"Sync Participants"** to fetch all entries
4. Review the participant list
5. Click **"Execute Draw"** when ready
6. Winners are selected randomly and displayed instantly!

### Step 5: Announce Your Winners

1. View the winner details on the results page
2. Download the winner certificate (proof of fairness)
3. Contact winners via their social media profiles
4. Share the results with your audience

## Best Practices

### Before Your Contest
1. **Clear Rules**: Post clear contest rules on your social media post
2. **Entry Requirements**: Be specific about what participants must do
3. **Timeline**: Announce the draw date in advance
4. **Legal Compliance**: Ensure your contest complies with platform rules and local laws

### During Your Contest
1. **Engage**: Respond to comments and questions
2. **Monitor**: Check your Cleack dashboard for participant stats
3. **No Changes**: Don't change contest rules after people have entered

### After Your Contest
1. **Quick Results**: Execute the draw as soon as possible after the deadline
2. **Transparency**: Share the winner announcement immediately
3. **Proof**: Provide the Cleack certificate as proof of fairness
4. **Follow Up**: Contact winners promptly and set a response deadline

## Common First-Time Questions

**Q: How long does it take to sync participants?**
A: Usually 30 seconds to 2 minutes, depending on the number of comments.

**Q: Can I run a draw on a private account?**
A: No, the post must be public for Cleack to access comments.

**Q: What if I don't have enough credits?**
A: You'll be prompted to purchase more credits before executing the draw.

**Q: Can I cancel a draw?**
A: Yes, you can cancel draft or active draws before execution. Credits are refunded.

**Q: What if a winner doesn't respond?**
A: You can run a "redraw" to select a replacement winner at a reduced cost.

**Q: Are the draws really random?**
A: Yes! We use cryptographically secure random number generation. Each draw includes a certificate with the random seed for verification.

## Troubleshooting

### "Cannot connect to Instagram"
- Make sure you're logged into the correct Instagram account
- Try clearing your browser cache and reconnecting
- Ensure you granted all required permissions

### "Post not found"
- Verify the post URL is correct
- Ensure the post is public, not private
- Check that the post hasn't been deleted

### "No participants found"
- Make sure the post has comments
- Verify your filters aren't too restrictive
- Try syncing participants again after a few minutes

### "Insufficient credits"
- Purchase more credits from Settings ‚Üí Billing
- Or upgrade to a Pro subscription for monthly credits
