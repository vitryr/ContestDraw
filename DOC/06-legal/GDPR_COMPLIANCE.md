## 3. DPO Responsibilities

### Statutory Duties (GDPR Article 39)

The DPO is responsible for:

#### A. Informing and Advising

- **Company:** Inform ContestDraw management and employees of GDPR obligations
- **Training:** Organize regular GDPR training sessions for staff
- **Advice:** Provide advice on Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs)
- **Consultations:** Be consulted on all data protection matters

#### B. Monitoring Compliance

- **Audits:** Conduct regular GDPR compliance audits (quarterly)
- **Policies:** Review and update data protection policies
- **Documentation:** Maintain records of processing activities (ROPA)
- **DPIAs:** Oversee Data Protection Impact Assessments
- **Breach Management:** Monitor data breach response procedures

#### C. Cooperation with Supervisory Authority

- **CNIL Contact:** Serve as point of contact for French CNIL
- **Reporting:** Report data breaches within 72 hours (when required)
- **Inquiries:** Respond to CNIL inquiries and requests
- **Consultations:** Facilitate consultations with CNIL

#### D. Acting as Contact Point

- **Data Subjects:** Handle data subject requests (access, deletion, etc.)
- **Questions:** Answer questions related to data rights
- **Complaints:** Receive and process data protection complaints
- **Documentation:** Document all interactions

## 4. DPO Independence

### Independence Safeguards

**GDPR Article 38(3):** The DPO shall not receive any instructions regarding the exercise of their tasks.

**ContestDraw ensures DPO independence through:**

1. **Direct Reporting:** DPO reports directly to CEO (not to Marketing or Product)
2. **No Conflicts of Interest:** DPO does not hold positions that determine processing purposes/means
3. **Protected Status:** Cannot be dismissed or penalized for performing DPO duties
4. **Budget Autonomy:** DPO has dedicated budget for training, tools, and consultations
5. **Resources:** Adequate time and resources to perform DPO tasks
6. **External Support:** Access to external legal counsel when needed

### Conflict of Interest Prevention

**DPO cannot simultaneously hold these positions:**
- ‚ùå Chief Technology Officer (CTO)
- ‚ùå Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)
- ‚ùå Head of Product
- ‚ùå Head of IT/Security (unless purely advisory)

**Current Safeguards:**
- ‚úÖ DPO is a dedicated role (not combined with conflicting positions)
- ‚úÖ No involvement in decisions on processing purposes/means
- ‚úÖ Independent voice in management meetings
